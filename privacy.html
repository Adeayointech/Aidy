Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aidy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Aidy mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and profile photo when you sign in with Google
  • Reminders and Tasks: Task titles, descriptions, dates, times, and categories you create
  • Chat Messages: Conversations you have with the AI assistant
  • Contact Information: Phone numbers you associate with call reminders

2.2 Gmail Data (Optional)

When you choose to connect your Gmail account:

  • We access your Gmail inbox to read emails for AI-assisted replies
  • We can send emails on your behalf when you use the email composition feature
  • Gmail data is only accessed when you explicitly use email features
  • We do not store your email content on our servers

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction patterns
  • Error Logs: Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Aidy service
  • Process your reminders, tasks, and notifications
  • Enable AI-powered features and natural language understanding
  • Compose and send emails when you request
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

4. AI Processing

Aidy uses Groq AI services to process your messages and provide intelligent responses. When you interact with the AI assistant:

  • Your messages are sent to Groq's API for processing
  • Conversation context is included to provide relevant responses
  • No personally identifiable information is shared beyond what's necessary for the service
  • AI processing follows Groq's privacy and security standards

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Reminders and tasks are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Account data is stored securely in Firebase with encryption
  • Security Measures: We use industry-standard security practices including encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Only you can access your data through authentication

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Google (authentication), Firebase (storage), Groq (AI processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

7. Gmail API Disclosure

Aidy's use of information received from Gmail APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements:

  • Gmail data is only used to provide and improve email features within Aidy
  • We do not transfer Gmail data to third parties except as required to provide the service
  • We do not use Gmail data for serving ads
  • Human review of Gmail data only occurs with your explicit consent for support purposes

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data stored in Aidy
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Revoke Permissions: Disconnect Gmail access at any time
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Opt-Out: Disable specific features or notifications

9. Data Retention

  • Reminders and tasks are retained until you delete them
  • Chat history is stored for improving AI responses
  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days

10. Children's Privacy

Aidy is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
